Dr. Stearns visited the Bowditch family to share important news.

the news about tom perry made nat very sad
Corrected: The news about Tom Perry made Nat very sad.

What needed fixing:
  • the → The — every sentence starts with a capital letter
  • tom → Tom — first name, proper noun
  • perry → Perry — last name, proper noun
  • nat → Nat — first name (nickname for Nathaniel), proper noun

Grammar · Day 3
Choosing Active Voice
✏️
Teacher models how to spot active vs. passive voice
🔑 Quick Reminder

Active voice: The subject does the action. “Nat solved the problem.”
Passive voice: The subject receives the action (often includes was/were/by). “The problem was solved by Nat.”

Each item below shows a passive sentence. Two versions are offered — click the one that rewrites it in active voice.

Passive: “The arithmetic problem was solved by Nat in a matter of seconds.”

Which version is active?

Passive: “Important news was brought to the Bowditch family by Dr. Stearns.”

Which version is active?

Passive: “The pieces of the expectation slip were scattered on the water by Nat.”

Which version is active?

✅ You can spot active voice in the wild now. Strong writers always put the doer first.

Alex thinks about what categories of evidence will appear in the book.
